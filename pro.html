Voice AI with Hand gestures interaction

This project aims to develop a touch-free human-computer interaction system, integrating hand gestures for cursor control and a voice assistant for browser navigation. The virtual mouse provides an alternative to physical mice in situations where they are impractical or unavailable. The voice assistant enhances accessibility, especially in environments like hospitals, old age homes, or during health crises like COVID-19, minimizing the need for device touch. The system prioritizes user-friendly design, aiming for practical applicability in diverse real-world scenarios.
